Our Story

Turning the Ordinary
into the Unforgettable

5+ Years of Magic

Born from a love of Agadir's breathtaking coastline, Nour To Sunset is a boutique animation and event planning studio rooted in Tamraght and Aourir — two of Morocco's most beautiful coastal villages.

We design intimate, bespoke experiences directly on the beach — where the Atlantic breeze, golden sand, and dramatic sunsets become the backdrop for the most important moments of your life.

Every setup we create is entirely handcrafted: from floral arches and candlelit picnics to decorated boats in the marina, each event is a unique work of art tailored entirely to you.
